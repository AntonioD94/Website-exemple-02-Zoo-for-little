Forests

Forests are diverse ecosystems that can be categorized into various types, including tropical, temperate, and boreal forests.
Each type of forest harbors a distinct set of flora and fauna.

Savannahs

Savannahs are vast grassland ecosystems characterized by a mix of grasses and scattered trees.
They are home to a diverse range of wildlife adapted to the open terrain.

Tundra

The tundra is a harsh and cold biome characterized by its low temperatures, permafrost, and limited vegetation.
Despite these challenging conditions, various animals have adapted to survive in this unique environment.
